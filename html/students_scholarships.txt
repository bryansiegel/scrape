<h1>Student Scholarships</h1>
<h2>Millennium Scholarship</h2>
<ul>
<li>Up to $10,000 to attend college in Nevada for eligible students. Click the link below to review the Fact Sheet.</li>
<li>CCSD will submit eligible students automatically, and an award package will be sent via US mail to your house in the summer when you graduate.</li>
<li>Students and parents can track eligibility in the Infinite Campus Parent/Student Portal.</li>
<li><a href="https://www.nevadatreasurer.gov/uploadedFiles/treasurernvgov/content/GGMS/Features/GGMS_High_School_Flyer-12_2020.pdf" target="_blank">Millennium Scholarship (PDF)</a></li>
</ul>
<h2>Nevada Promise Scholarship</h2>
<ul>
<li><a href="https://www.csn.edu/csns-nevada-promise-scholarship" target="_blank">Nevada Promise Scholarship</a></li>
<li>702-651-7500</li>
<li>The Nevada Promise Scholarship is a unique scholarship established by the Nevada Legislature for Nevada high school graduates to attend community college.</li>
<li>As a last-dollar scholarship, the Nevada Promise Scholarship can cover up to three years of tuition and other mandatory fees not covered by other gift aid.</li>
<li>Students must apply during the application period (August through October), and keep current on the ongoing application steps.</li>
</ul>

<h2>Other College Financial Aid Resources to Consider</h2>
<p><strong>Western Undergraduate Exchange (WUE)</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><a href="https://www.wiche.edu/tuition-savings/wue/" target="_blank">Western Undergraduate Exchange (WUE) Tuition Saving Program</a></li>
<li>The Western Undergraduate Exchange (WUE) is an agreement among 16 member states and territories, through which 160+ participating public colleges and universities provide steep nonresident tuition savings for Nevada students.</li>
<li>Through WUE, eligible students can choose from hundreds of undergraduate programs outside their home state, and pay no more than 150 percent of that institution’s resident tuition rate. </li>
</ul>
<p><strong>QuestBridge National College Match</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><a href="https://www.questbridge.org/high-school-students/national-college-match" target="_blank">QuestBridge National College Match</a></li>
<li><a href="https://www.youtube.com/user/QuestBridge " target="_blank">A Top College is Possible with QuestBridge</a></li>
<li>The QuestBridge National College Match makes a top college possible for high-achieving students from low-income backgrounds. Match Scholarship Recipients are granted early admission to one of QuestBridge's 45 college partners with a full four-year scholarship, worth over $200,000. </li>
</ul>
