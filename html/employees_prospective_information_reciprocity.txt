<h1>Reciprocity</h1>
<p>Please visit the Nevada Department of Education's (NDE) website at <a class="xlink" href="http://nvteachers.doe.nv.gov/Reciprocity.htm" target="_blank">http://nvteachers.doe.nv.gov/Reciprocity.htm</a>   regarding reciprocity that Nevada has with other states.  Testing requirements for the NDE vary widely depending on your area of licensure, highest degree, years of contracted teaching experience, coursework, and/or passing test scores.  NDE testing requirements are detailed on their website at <br/>
<a class="xlink" href="http://nvteachers.doe.nv.gov/Testing_Requirements.htm" target="_blank">http://nvteachers.doe.nv.gov/Testing_Requirements.htm</a>
</p>
