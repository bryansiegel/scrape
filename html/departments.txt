<h2>Clark County School District Units and Departments</h2>
<p>The Clark County School District (CCSD) is organized into specialized units that oversee key areas of district operations, with each unit made up of dedicated departments that carry out essential services. Units provide leadership and direction in areas such as academics, finance, human resources, communications, operations, and student support, while departments within each unit focus on specific responsibilities like budgeting, facilities management, recruitment, media relations, technology, and more. Together, this structure ensures that CCSD runs efficiently and effectively, supporting students, families, employees, and the community.</p>
