<h2>
	Mission Statement</h2>
<p>
	Our mission is to independently evaluate the effectiveness of the District's activities and provide consulting services designed to add value and improve the District's operations.</p>
<hr/>
<h2>
	Internal Audit Department Charter</h2>
<p>
	Clark County School District Regulation 3420 establishes the purpose of the department, the authority under which it operates, and the responsibilities of the department.</p>
<p>
	All internal audits are conducted and reported on in accordance with the Institute of Internal Auditors Code of Ethics and the International Standards for the Professional Practice of Internal Auditing.</p>
