<p>The goal is for all schools to be audited over time. High Schools and Middle Schools are audited every 18 months-2 years. Elementary Schools are audited at least every 3 years.</p>
<p>
All school audits will receive one of the ratings listed below:</p>
<p>
<b>CLEAN</b> - No reportable issues.
</p>
<p><b>NORMAL</b> - Common compliance issues.</p>
<p>
<b>PRIORITY (P1)</b> - Deficiencies have been noted in some areas that need to be revisited sooner than what the regular rotation allows.</p>
<p>
<b>AT RISK</b> - The school has a general lack of controls and non-adherence to District policies and procedures, and assurance cannot be provided.</p>
<p>
<b>Clean and Normal</b> rated school audits are placed back in the regular audit rotation. <b>P1</b> and <b>AT RISK</b> rated school audits are reaudited within the following 6 to 12 months.</p>
<p>
To receive a copy of a school audit report, please contact Janette Scott at (702) 799-6360 ext. 5070.
</p>
