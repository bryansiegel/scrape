<h1>
	Doing Business with CCSD</h1>
<p>
	Clark County School District encourages and promotes all suppliers in doing business with CCSD.</p>
<p>
	District contracting and purchasing activities are divided up as follows:</p>
<h2>
	Public Works</h2>
<p>
	Capital Program Office<br>
	1180 Military Tribute Place<br>
	Henderson, NV 89074<br/>
	(702) 799-8710</br></br></p>
<h2>
	Goods, Commodities, and Non-Construction Services</h2>
<p>
	Purchasing &amp; Warehousing Department<br/>
	4212 Eucalyptus Avenue<br/>
	Las Vegas, NV 89121<br/>
	(702) 799-5225<br/>
	(702) 799-5504 fax</p>
<p>
	The Purchasing Department does not pre-qualify suppliers nor maintain a bidders' list. The bidders' list is maintained by the on-line hosting system at <a href="http://www.demandstar.com/" target="_blank">www.demandstar.com</a>. Download and submittal opportunities for competitive efforts can be found at <a href="https://ccsd.bonfirehub.com">https://ccsd.bonfirehub.com</a>. All formal competitive efforts are advertised in the classified section of the Review-Journal.</p>
