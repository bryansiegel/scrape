<h1>
	Internal Items Available for District Use Only </h1>
<h2>
	Internal Used and Surplus Furniture</h2>
<p>
	See <a href="https://ccsd.net/resources/purchasing-warehousing/used-surplus-furn-v04.pdf" style='color: rgb(23, 113, 183); transition: all 0.1s ease 0s; line-height: 27.2px; font-family: proxima-nova, "Helvetica Neue", Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 17px;'>Internal Used and Surplus Furniture Program</a></p>
<h2>
	Items Available for District Use</h2>
<ul>
<li>
		Items must be used for district purposes only</li>
<li>
		District employees with AD usernames may express interest in items</li>
<li>
		Items must be used for district business purpose on district property</li>
<li>
		District employees must view items by appointment before selection<br>
		Call (702) 799-5225 and ask for the used furniture warehouse<br>
		Visitor hours: 6:00–11:00 a.m., Monday-Friday, excluding holidays<br/>
		For safety: Wear close-toe shoes and appropriate attire, such as long pants. Visitors with open-toe shoes or sandals will not be allowed in the warehouse</br></br></li>
<li>
		Site administrator will be contacted to approve any item prior to pick-up or delivery</li>
<li>
		Awarded items can be picked up or can be delivered to school or department site using the <a href="https://prd-wd.ccsd.net:8121/sap/bc/webdynpro/sap/zmm_ship1_req?sap-client=400">Shipping 1 form</a>. Please see <em><a href="https://ccsd.net/resources/purchasing-warehousing/how-to-use-the-shipping-1-form.pdf">How to use the SHIPPING-1 form</a></em> for instructions.</li>
<li>
		No personal use allowed</li>
</ul>
