<p>The following information is provided for administrative and professional-technical employees who are currently on Column H/H1 or above. Hours may only be accrued while on H/H1 or above for column advancement to I/I1, J/J1, and K/K1 on the Administrative and Professional-Technical Salary Schedule.</p>
<p>Column Advancement Information sessions are listed in the ELMS (Learning Activity keyword “Column Advancement Information”).</p>
<hr/>
<h2>Annual Deadlines</h2>
<p><strong>March 31</strong> – Deadline to submit Request for Column Advancement for the next contract year. If an administrative or professional-technical employee fails to comply with this deadline, then the employee will not advance a column the next contract year. The Request for Column Advancement is provided in this link: <a contenteditable="false" href="https://forms.gle/T6BiMvN3cfFWFqQT8" style="cursor: pointer;" target="_blank">HERE</a>.</p>
<p><strong>May 15</strong> – Deadline for accruing professional learning hours for column advancement at the beginning of the next contract year (July 1 or August 1), with the exception of college coursework. The deadline for accruing professional learning hours for college coursework is May 31. Any professional learning hours earned after these deadlines does not count toward advancement for the following contract year.</p>
<p><strong>June 15</strong> – Deadline for submitting professional learning documentation in the Clark County School District (CCSD) Enterprise Learning Management System (ELMS) for column advancement at the beginning of the next contract year. Official university transcripts must be received by the Professional Growth System (PGS) and Nevada Education Performance Framework (NEPF) Department via e-transcript from the university at <a contenteditable="false" href="mailto:AdminSalary@nv.ccsd.net" style="cursor: pointer;">AdminSalary@nv.ccsd.net</a> or at the following physical address:<br/>
Clark County School District<br/>
Human Resources – Attn: PGS and NEPF Department<br/>
2832 E. Flamingo Road<br/>
Las Vegas, NV 89121</p>
<p>For employees who submitted their Request for Column Advancement by March 31, CCSD shall provide a response in CCSD ELMS to correctly submitted professional learning documentation within eight (8) weeks from the date of submission. If an employee is short hours, the employee shall be afforded an opportunity to resubmit additional documentation of any hours earned by May 15.</p>

<h2>Documentation Submission Deadline and Tutorials</h2>
<p>June 15 Submission Deadline:<br/>
All documentation for professional learning hours accrued by May 15 are due in CCSD ELMS by June 15. Any professional learning submissions timestamped on or after 12:00 a.m. on June 16 will not count toward advancement for the following contract year. Training sessions for submitting professional learning hours can be found in CCSD ELMS, keywords “Column Advancement Information.”</p>
