<p>Schools in the Clark County School District are committed to creating welcoming, supportive environments where every student can thrive. With a wide range of programs and opportunities available, families can explore schools that best fit their child’s needs and interests. Whether you’re searching for a neighborhood school, a magnet program, or a career and technical academy, CCSD makes it easy to find your school and connect with the resources that will guide your student toward success.</p>
