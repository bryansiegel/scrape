<h2>School Accountability Reports</h2>
<p><a href="/schools/accountability-reports">School Accountability Reports</a> contain detailed information about schools in the Clark County School District, as well as charter schools. Annual reports are prepared in compliance with the federal No Child Left Behind Act (NCLB) and Nevada Revised Statute (NRS) 385. Accountability reports are used by teachers and administrators as they develop academic plans to increase achievement of all students.</p>

<h2>School Performance Plans</h2>
<p><a href="https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1v2I_SvexstJk2AGA3THUyvqoB7a0W4Ol">School Performance Plans</a> contain detailed information about schools in the Clark County School District, as well as charter schools. Plans are prepared in compliance with the Nevada Revised Statutes (NRS) 385A.650, 388G.700, and 388G.710. School Performance Plans identify goals and improvement strategies, along with detailed action steps designed to increase the achievement of all students.</p>
