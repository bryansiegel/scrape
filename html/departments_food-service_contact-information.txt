<h1>
	Contact Information</h1>
<h2>
	Contact your School Cafeteria</h2>
<p>
	Please contact your child's school cafeteria Manager or Senior Food Service Worker for any of the following questions:</p>
<ul>
<li>
		If you have a question about the eligibility of your child</li>
<li>
		If you have a question about your child's account</li>
<li>
		If you want to pay for your student's meals in advance (up to the end of a school year is permitted)</li>
<li>
		If you would like information about working for the Food Service Department</li>
</ul>
<p>
	You can find your schools contact information on the <a href="http://ccsd.net/schools/contact-information/">District Directory</a>.</p>
