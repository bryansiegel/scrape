<h1>Welcome to Clark County School District</h1>
<h2>The Destination District</h2>
<p>What does it mean to be a Destination District? It means that students, families, and employees choose the Clark County School District because they believe in what is possible here!</p>
<p><strong>We will reach our Destination when we see:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Increased student outcomes and enrollment;</li>
<li>Employees who feel respected, safe, fulfilled, and committed; and</li>
<li>Community stakeholders who feel included and support CCSD’s priorities.</li>
</ul>
<p>CCSD leaders are laser-focused on student success with a Destination in sight - a Destination in which every student is prepared for a bright future! We will invite you to join us every step along the way.</p>
</div>
<div style="padding-top:15px;">
<p><span style="font-size:30px; font-weight:bold;">Jhone Ebert</span><br/>
<strong>Superintendent</strong></p>

<h3>Operation: Destination District</h3>
<p>CCSD is at a pivotal moment for students, families, staff members, and our entire community as we work to transform our schools to meet the needs of today’s students and workforce.</p>
<p>Superintendent Ebert’s 100-Day plan made strides in improving CCSD’s climate and culture. We’re building on increased student achievement with our focus on the “Core Four” academic priorities.</p>
<h5>Up next</h5>
<p>We are co-designing a strategic plan to set goals and allocate resources, a Facilities Master Plan to ensure every student has access to an up-to-date school with the technology and programming they need to succeed, and an efficiency study to ensure every taxpayer dollar is maximized.</p>
