<h1>CCSD Professional Growth System (PGS)</h1>

<p>The following information is provided for teachers and other licensed employees who are accruing Contact Units (CUs) for column advancement.</p>
<p>PGS Information sessions are listed in the ELMS (Learning Activity keyword “PGS Information”).</p>
<p>CCSD Title I schools that are eligible for the 2 year advancement track are provided here: <a href="https://drive.google.com/file/d/1HUSPyCdMSz-HbKi-rgausKX127qk-aEg/view" target="_blank">PGS Title I Schools – 2 Year Track</a>.</p>
<hr/>
<h2>Annual Deadlines</h2>
<p><strong>June 1</strong> – Deadline to submit the Request for Column Advancement for the next contract year. If a licensed employee fails to comply with this deadline, then the employee will not advance a column the next contract year. This form opens in early March and closes on the June 1 deadline. The Request for Column Advancement is provided in this link: 2025-2026 Advancement: <a href="https://forms.gle/iGbtT8FPeV6VdTPs6" target="_blank">HERE</a>.</p>
<p><strong>August 31</strong> – Deadline to complete all requirements for all hours/coursework being used for the column advancement for the current contract year.
</p>
<p><strong>October 1</strong> – Deadline to submit required documentation within the ELMS for <u>ALL</u> activities completed during the advancement cycle is October 1 of the year the employee is advancing. Submissions of activities completed during a previous advancement window will not be accepted. Official university transcripts must be received by the Professional Growth System department via e-transcript from the university at pgs@nv.ccsd.net or at the following physical address:</p>
<p>
Clark County School District<br/>
Human Resources Division – Attn: PGS Department<br/>
2832 E. Flamingo Road<br/>
Las Vegas, NV 89121
</p>
<p>Licensed employees submitting documentation for PGS advancement must know:</p>
<ul style="margin-left: 20px;">
<li>Employees must submit original, unaltered documentation in the ELMS as provided by the issuing organization.</li>
<li>Employees modifying documentation provided by the issuing organization and submitting altered documentation in the ELMS for PGS advancement may result in discipline up to and including termination.</li>
</ul>

<h3>PGS Reference Guide</h3>
<p>The Reference Guide contains the activities that may be used for CU accrual within the Professional Growth System:</p>
<ul style="margin-left: 20px;">
<li><a href="https://drive.google.com/file/d/10V5_yms7KG03dxRI0wJNwel0K7gGxwC-/view?usp=drive_link" target="_blank">9/1/23 PGS Reference Guide (Posted 5/1/24)</a></li>
<li><a href="https://drive.google.com/file/d/0By9bK5CEHAt0U1BUN0hUd3Y3N3c/view?resourcekey=0-dPbT-yfCaStDqyeG71Oa-A" target="_blank">10/16/17 PGS Reference Guide (Expired 5/1/24)</a></li>
<li><a href="https://docs.google.com/document/d/1FX6iMQSUPBpYPmad7Spc_IAmPqKzg-jfG7x0VikRUqY/edit?tab=t.0" target="_blank">CCSD and CCEA PGS Announcements Impacting Contact Unit Accrual</a></li>
</ul>
<p>The transition to the 9/1/23 PGS Reference Guide adheres to the following understandings: </p>
<ul style="margin-left: 20px;">
<li>Activities completed prior to 5/1/24 will accrue via the 10/16/17 PGS Reference Guide categories and maximums.</li>
<li>Activities completed on or after 5/1/24 will accrue via the 9/1/23 PGS Reference Guide categories and maximums.</li>
<li>If a licensed employee has accrued CUs in line with the 10/16/17 PGS Reference Guide maximums on or before 5/1/24 that exceed the 9/1/23 PGS Reference Guide maximums, then the employee will keep the higher amount of CUs accrued up to the higher category maximum. </li>
<li>If a licensed employee has not met or exceeded a 10/16/17 PGS Reference Guide category maximum based on activities completed through 5/1/24, then the employee's CU accrual will be held to the 9/1/23 PGS Reference Guide maximums. </li>
<li>Activities being claimed for CU accrual to a higher maximum (10/16/17 PGS Reference Guide) that are completed after the 5/1/24 posting of the 9/1/23 PGS Reference Guide will need the employee to also submit registration confirmation of the activity to ensure the activity was registered/paid for prior to the 9/1/23 PGS Reference Guide posting.</li>
</ul>

<h2>Documentation Submission Deadline and Tutorials</h2>
<p><strong>October 1 Submission Deadline</strong><br/>
All documentation for CUs accrued by August 31 are due in CCSD ELMS by October 1. Any CU submissions timestamped on or after 12:00 a.m. on October 2 will not count toward column advancement for the current contract year. Training sessions for submitting professional learning hours can be found in CCSD ELMS, keyword “PGS.”
</p><hr/>
