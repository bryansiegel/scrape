<h1>Board Policies &amp; Regulations</h1>
<h2>Notification of proposed changes to CCSD Policies and/or Regulations</h2>
<p>State statute requires the Board of School Trustees adopt a
                          Notice of Intent when it considers the adoption, amendment, or repeal of a
                          policy or regulation. The Notice of Intent provides the public thirteen (13)
                          days notice prior to final action by the board.</p>
<p>Comments by the public are welcome.</p>
<h2>Policies/Regulations Under Consideration</h2>
<p>The public is welcome to comment when policies/regulations are under consideration
                        by the superintendent for adoption, amendment, or repeal. The policies/regulations
                        listed in this category will be posted for a period of five (5) business
                        days prior to submission to the Board of School Trustees.</p>
<p><strong>No policies or regulations are scheduled at this time.</strong></p>
