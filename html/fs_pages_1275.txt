<h2>CCSD-sponsored mental health services</h2>
<h3>CCSD-sponsored mental health services available to all CCSD students.</h3>
<p><strong><a href="https://my.hazel.co/ccsd/info" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">Hazel Health</a>:</strong> CCSD-sponsored agency that provides virtual therapy sessions at school or home. <strong>(702) 718-0123</strong><br/>
<strong><a href="https://www.caresolace.com/site/ccsd" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">Care Solace</a>:</strong> CCSD-sponsored agency to find counseling services. <strong>(888) 515-0595</strong><br/>
<strong><a href="https://www.invocompanies.com/impact" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">IMPACT</a></strong>: CCSD-sponsored agency that provides mental health for students at school while addressing key behavioral challenges. <strong>1-866-468-6471</strong><br/>
<strong>Mobile Crisis Response Team:</strong> Assist students with thoughts of suicide. <strong>(702) 486-7865</strong><br/>
<strong>SafeVoice:</strong> <strong>1-833-216-SAFE (7233)</strong> for anonymous reporting<br/>
<strong><a href="https://www.miraclemindstherapy.org/" target="_blank">United Citizens Foundation</a></strong>: CCSD-sponsored agency that provides mental health sessions at school for students. <strong>(702) 888-6300</strong></p>
