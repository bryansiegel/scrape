<h1 class="fsPageTitle">Human Resources</h1>
<div class="fsPageLayout fsLayout fsOneColumnLayout fsStyleAutoclear" data-use-new="true" id="fsEl_11878">

<div class="fsElement fsContent" data-use-new="true" id="fsEl_11882">
<div class="fsElementContent">
<p>The Human Resources Division is dedicated to the recruitment and retention of a quality workforce in support of student success. The District employs over 40,000 people, including teachers, support professionals, administrators, and school police, who provide services to over 300,000 students.</p>
<p><a class="button-7" href="https://www.ccsd.net/uploaded/themes/default_25/directory/org-chart/ccsd-org-chart.pdf">Organizational Chart</a></p>

<div class="fsElement fsContent" data-use-new="true" id="fsEl_12103">
<div class="fsElementContent">
<p>As we work to hire and retain a qualified staff, the goal of the Human Resources Division is to provide excellent customer service and to build a team of professionals who are passionate about the success of students, and who are willing to dedicate the necessary time and resources to meet the needs of a large, diverse, urban school district.</p>
<p>Students in Clark County are fortunate to live in a community that supports and values their overall success, which begins with employing the finest staff of administrators, teachers, support professionals, and school police.</p>
