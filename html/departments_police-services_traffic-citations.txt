<h1>
	Traffic Citations</h1>
<p>
	You have received a traffic citation that is considered either a <em>civil infraction</em> or a <em>criminal offense;</em> the following information will guide you on how to resolve the citation.</p>
<p>
	Your citation will have the words "CIVIL" or "CRIMINAL" printed in the top right corner of your citation, please select the coordinating option below:</p>
<h3>
	           <a href="https://ccsd.net/departments/police-services/civil-infractions"><u>Civil Infraction</u></a>    <em>OR </em>   <a href="https://ccsd.net/departments/police-services/criminal-offense"><u>Criminal Offense</u></a></h3>
<p>
	*Click <strong><a href="https://ccsd.net/departments/police-services/civil-vs-criminal">HERE</a></strong> for a Quick Guide on knowing the difference.</p>
