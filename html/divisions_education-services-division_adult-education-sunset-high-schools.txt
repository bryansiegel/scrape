<h2>
	Adult Education</h2>
<h3>
	Concurrent and/or Full-Time Enrollment</h3>
<p>
	Adult Education is an educational program designed to serve adults and out of school youths, 18 years of age or older, who desire to earn a high school diploma or obtain a Nevada State Certificate of High School Equivalency. There is no fee for out of school youth and adults. The program also serves students enrolled in comprehensive or non-traditional schools (concurrent students) who desire to make up high school credits..For additional information call 702-799-8650.</p>
<p>
<strong><a href="https://www.clarkcountyadulteducation.net/" target="_blank">Our Web Site</a></strong></p>
<h2>
	High School Equivalency Certificate (HSE)</h2>
<div align="center">
	(Nevada State Certificate of High School Equivalency)</div>
<p>
	In the absence of a high school diploma, the High School Equivalency Test (HSE) is used as a measure of an individual's basic competencies in the areas of English (writing and literature), social studies, science, and mathematics. Individuals who desire to pursue a Nevada State Certificate of High School Equivalency must meet the following requirements:</p>
<ul>
	1. 17 years of age or older w/parental permission (16 year olds require administrative approval)<br>
	2. Officially withdrawn from school;<br>
	3. Registration;<br/>
	4. HSE testing fee of $65.00; and<br/>
	5. Proof of identity (i.e. valid drivers license or DMV identification card).
</br></br></ul>
<p>
	Counseling services are mandatory and are available to all enrolled and prospective students. Students may be counseled on a walk-in or an appointment basis at various locations throughout Clark County. For information regarding the HSE program call 702-799-8650 extension 5306.</p>
<p>
<a href="https://clarkcountyadulteducation.net/">Website</a></p>
