<h1>
	Approved Engineers and Architects</h1>
<p>
	The selection, procurement, and administration of architects, engineers, and related professional service providers for Clark County School District projects are governed by applicable Nevada Revised Statutes and Clark County School District (CCSD) Regulation 7211. The Clark County School District encourages participation by women-owned, disadvantaged, and minority firms.</p>
<p>
	The selection of architects and engineers to be recommended to the Board of School Trustees is based on established qualification and selection criteria.</p>
<p>
	The <a href="http://facilities.ccsd.net">Clark County School District Facilities Division</a> uses the board-approved lists of architects and engineers to select the most appropriate firm for each project.</p>
<p>
	Other construction-related services are procured via Requests for Proposals (RFPs) which are published in the Las Vegas Review-Journal classified section and in applicable trade publications.</p>
