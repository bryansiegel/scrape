<h2>What are examples of partnership opportunities with CCSD?</h2>
<p>CCSD provides many opportunities for partnership, including:</p>
<p><strong>Support to students</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Provide incentives to students who improve their attendance or academics (e.g., student store donations, movie tickets, gift cards, certificates)</li>
<li>Provide informal or supplemental resources to support instruction</li>
<li>Sponsor student contests or scholarships</li>
<li>Collect/donate school supplies and backpacks</li>
<li>Collect/donate new clothing or shoes aligned with the student attire program</li>
<li>Donate to a school’s food pantry to assist students and their families</li>
<li>Sponsor a student recognition event</li>
<li>Adopt students and their families for a holiday giving program</li>
<li>Provide volunteers to mentor or tutor students</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Support to teachers</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Organizations can provide volunteers to chaperone a school event (e.g., Field Day, field trip)</li>
<li>Sponsor Teacher Appreciation Week treats or events through offers, discounts, or promotions (e.g., breakfast, gift cards, certificates)</li>
<li>Provide monetary or in-kind donations for classroom or teacher materials</li>
<li>Recognize high-performing or exemplary staff (e.g., gift cards, certificates)</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Support to schools</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Provide volunteers to read during Nevada Reading Week</li>
<li>Provide volunteers to assist with and/or table at school events</li>
<li>Provide supplies or volunteers for a school/classroom refresh (e.g., painting, furniture)</li>
<li>Provide a monetary donation to cover the cost of fingerprinting parents who cannot afford it but want to volunteer at the school</li>
</ul>
</div>
