<h1>#ReadWithMeCCSD</h1>
<h2 style="margin-bottom:8px; margin-left: -15px;">#ReadWithMeCCSD Summer Reading Photo
								Contest</h2>
<p>Share photos of your child or family reading this summer and you could win an Amazon gift
								card!</p>
<img align="left" alt="Summer Reading Contest" src="images/photo-contest.jpg" style="padding: 15px; padding-right: 35px;"/>
<p style="padding-top:45px; text-align:left;">Post photos, including student’s first name
								and CCSD school, on social media (Instagram, Twitter, and Facebook - settings must allow
								public view) using hashtag #ReadWithMeCCSD. Or photos can be submitted via email to <a href="mailto:communications@nv.ccsd.net">communications@nv.ccsd.net</a>. Your photo
								might be selected to win an Amazon gift card!</p>
<div style="margin-left: 15px;">
<ul style="text-align: left !important;">
<p style="margin-left: 15px;"><br>
<li>Post a picture of your child or family reading in an interesting place.</li>
<li>Post a picture of your child or family reading something interesting, other
											than a book.</li>
<li>Post a picture of your child or family reading to a sibling, pet, or other
											interesting buddy.</li>
</br></p>

<p style="text-align:left;">Any picture of your child or family reading could be a winner!</p>
<p>Bring your books on break! The Clark County School District (CCSD) encourages students to
							continue building their reading skills over the summer months. Students should read
							throughout the day, all summer long, at least 30 minutes a day. Students can read books,
							magazines, comic books … any reading counts!</p>
<p>Remember, reading includes much more than just books. Here are <a href="pdf/100-Ways-to-Raise-a-READER.pdf" target="_blank">100 ways</a> to make reading
							more enjoyable and motivate your children to read. Parents and guardians are invited to
							learn strategies for establishing consistent family reading routines from this <a href="https://engage.ccsd.net/uflelementary/" target="_blank">three part video
								series</a>.</p>

<h2 style="margin-bottom:8px;">Reading Resources</h2>
<p>The resources below will help parents or guardians and students find reading
									materials that will engage their interest and help spur critical thinking. Please
									recognize that using the resources below is voluntary. Not every book selected suits
									every student. Parents or guardians should be engaged in the selection and
									discussion of reading material to ensure appropriateness of topics and texts for
									their students.</p>
<h3>Destiny Discover</h3>
<p>Access your school library and online resources including eBooks, online databases,
									and open educational resources.</p>
<p><strong>Access Destiny Discover</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Log in to <a href="https://clever.ccsd.net" target="_blank">clever.ccsd.net</a>
										with the student’s Active Directory or CCSD Google credentials.</li>
<li>Click or tap the Follett Destiny Discover logo within the “Apps” section.</li>
</ul>
<h3>Nevada Young Reader's Award Books</h3>
<p>Check out books suggested by Nevada’s students. Go to <a href="https://nevadalibraries.org/NYRA" target="_blank">nevadalibraries.org/NYRA</a>.
								</p>
<h3>American Library Association Children and Young Adults Awards Lists</h3>
<p>Check out books from the American Library Association Children and Young Adults
									Awards Lists. Go to <a href="https://www.ala.org/alsc/awardsgrants/notalists/ncb" target="_blank">ALA American Library Association</a>.
								</p>
<h3>More Summer Fun</h3>
<p>The Vegas PBS Learning Neighborhood Van brings the learning to you and your family!
									Join them at free family engagement workshops, educational pop-ups, and community
									events in libraries and neighborhoods throughout Southern Nevada over the summer.
									Their staff will show you how to learn anytime, anywhere, with the help of your
									favorite PBS KIDS media! Visit <a href="https://www.vegaspbs.org/rtl/van/" target="_blank">The Vegas PBS Learning Neighborhood Van</a> to find out where
									they will be next!</p>
<p><strong>The Clark County School District Board of Trustees, Superintendent, and staff
										hope you have a safe and happy summer, with lots of time to read!</strong></p>
