<h1 class="fsPageTitle">Parents &amp; Students</h1>
<div class="fsPageLayout fsLayout fsOneColumnLayout fsStyleAutoclear" data-use-new="true" id="fsEl_9860">
<div class="fsDiv fsStyleAutoclear" id="fsEl_9861">
<div class="fsElement fsLayout fsTwoColumnWideLeftLayout sub-hero-border fsStyleAutoclear" data-use-new="true" id="fsEl_207439">
<div class="fsDiv fsStyleTwoThirds fsStyleColumn fsStyleColumn-1 fsStyleAutoclear" id="fsEl_207440">
<div class="fsElement fsContent" data-use-new="true" id="fsEl_207443">
<div class="fsElementContent">
<div><p>To help you more effectively monitor the progress of your child, the Clark County School District provides a web based system. This system will provide detailed, real-time information, including: Calendar, Schedules, Attendance, Grades, Health (Immunizations Only), Assignments, Academic Planner, Academic Progress, Fees/Payments, To Do Lists, Reports, District/School Notices, Cafeteria Balance, School Meals Application.</p>
<p><a class="button-7" href="http://campusportal.ccsd.net" target="_blank">Visit Campus Parent/Student</a></p>

<div class="fsElementContent">
<p>The Campus Parent and Campus Student mobile apps provide a fast and convenient way for parents and students to check grades, assignments, schedule, attendance and daily planner information on their IOS or Android device. The apps are available to our parents and students at no cost and can be downloaded directly from the iTunes App Store, Google play store or from the links provided below.</p>
