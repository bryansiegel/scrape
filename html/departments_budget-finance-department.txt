<h1>
	Budget Department</h1>
<h2>
	Departmental Mission Statement</h2>
<p>
	The Budget and Analysis Department exists to assist the Clark County School District (CCSD) and comply with the financial laws governing public education in Clark County as directed in the Nevada Revised Statutes (NRS) and Nevada Administrative Code (NAC).</p>
