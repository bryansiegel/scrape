<h1 style="margin: 0 0 5px;">Emily Stevens</h1>
<h2 style="margin: 0 0 5px;">Member</h2>
<div class="trustee-bio" style="background-color: #fbfbfb; border: 1px solid #e3e3e3;"><p>Emily Stevens is a native of Las Vegas, having spent most of her life in the Southern Nevada community. She and her partner have six children and stay very busy with all the fun and activities of having a large family.</p>

<div style="padding-top: 25px;padding-bottom: 20px;">
   						Emily has been in financial services for over 6 years but has over 20 years of experience in sales, management, and marketing. She joined Rize Credit Union in August 2018 as their Community Development Executive in Southern Nevada. Her role has expanded into a Vice President position where she leads sales and service teams in multiple regions.
   				</div>
<span class="read-more-target">
<a id="p1"><div class="more-link">Read More</div></a>
<div class="hiddendiv" id="scritta">
	Since working at the credit union, Emily has had the opportunity to teach financial literacy across the valley. Teaching instantly became a passion, and she has been an active member of the Southern Nevada Community. Emily currently sits on the board of the Academy of Finance at Clark High School and the Advisory Committee for Communities in Schools NV. Her passion for education and vast experience in business have led her to the school board position, where she strives to positively impact our children, families, educators, and community.
