<h3>Children’s Privacy</h3>
<p>This application is intended to be used by student and parents of Clark County School District. That means that children under the age of 13 are able to use the app. Because of that we must comply with COPPA. The information we are going to collect from the students and/or parents are their name, phone number, and email. We will also use their geo-location but only when the app is being used. Their location will not be stored anywhere. The information collected will be managed by the Clark County School District Transportation Department. The information with not be shared or disclosed outside the school district.</p>
