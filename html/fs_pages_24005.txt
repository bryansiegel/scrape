<h1 class="fsPageTitle">Secondary Summer School</h1>
<div class="fsPageLayout fsLayout fsOneColumnLayout fsStyleAutoclear" data-use-new="true" id="fsEl_213156">
<div class="fsDiv fsStyleAutoclear" id="fsEl_213157">
<div class="fsElement fsLayout fsTwoColumnWideLeftLayout sub-hero-border fsStyleAutoclear" data-use-new="true" id="fsEl_213159">
<div class="fsDiv fsStyleTwoThirds fsStyleColumn fsStyleColumn-1 fsStyleAutoclear" id="fsEl_213160">
<div class="fsElement fsContent" data-use-new="true" id="fsEl_213161">
<div class="fsElementContent">
<p>Secondary summer school for 2026 provides a critical opportunity for students to engage in in-person credit recovery at eight select campuses located throughout the valley. These sessions are specifically designed for current middle school and high school students to earn up to 0.5 credits per session through intensive, five-hour daily classes running from early June to mid-July. To participate in this program, parents must work directly with their child's school counselor to complete the registration process by May 27.</p>
