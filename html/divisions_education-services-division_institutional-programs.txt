<h1>
	Institutional Schools</h1>
<p>
	Phone: 702-799-8650</p>
<p>
	The Clark County School District provides educational programs for students who are no longer eligible, either temporarily or permanently, due to being incarcerated, for enrollment in comprehensive schools as well as the adjudicated youth of Clark County, Nevada. Upon release from incarceration, the student may become eligible for enrollment in a comprehensive school or in an educational program offered by the Education Services Division. Adjudicated students may be afforded educational instruction from Clark County School District programs at:</p>
