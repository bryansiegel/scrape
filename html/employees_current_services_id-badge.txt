<h1>Identification Badge</h1>
<h2>Photo Identification Badge Schedule</h2>
<p><strong>Per District Regulations 4100 and 4312</strong>, all regular status employees, substitute employees, temporary employees, contracted employees, and volunteers are required to display a photo identification badge during work hours prominently. Upon expiration or change in position, current District personnel may obtain a new identification badge by appointment only by contacting <a href="mailto:badges@nv.ccsd.net">badges@nv.ccsd.net</a> or 702-799-2846. Hours of operations are <strong>Monday through Friday, 7:30 a.m. to 4:30 p.m.</strong> at the Edward A. Greer Education Center, <a href="https://www.google.com/maps/place/Parking+lot,+2832+E+Flamingo+Rd,+Las+Vegas,+NV+89121/@36.1143939,-115.1149507,17z/data=!3m1!4b1!4m5!3m4!1s0x80c8c520cfb45983:0x8dbf5e06c0d5a1f1!8m2!3d36.1143939!4d-115.112762" target="_blank">2832 East Flamingo Road</a>. All new hires, substitute, temporary, contracted, volunteers, and Crisis Response Team/Threat Assessment personnel must obtain a photo identification badge at the Edward A. Greer Education Center.
</p>
<p>School volunteers are issued their initial badge at the Edward A. Greer Education Center. The site administrator can renew the badge the following year by affixing a renewal sticker.</p>
<p><strong>Food Service Personnel</strong> may renew at the Food Service Department located at 6350 E. Tropical Parkway or at the Edward A. Greer Education Center.</p>
<p><strong>Transportation Employees</strong> must renew at the Wallace Bus Yard located at 975 W.Welpman Way. Badges may be obtained Monday through Friday, from <strong>7 a.m. to 10 a.m. and 1 p.m. to 3 p.m.</strong> This schedule may vary, so please call (702) 799-6890 ext. 5046.</p>
<p><strong>Please note:</strong> Employees must provide picture identification at the time of service. Professional attire adhering to <strong>District Regulation 4280</strong> is required for the photo. Hats and sunglasses are not permitted. Only one badge will be issued per employee. Old badges must be forfeit. Nicknames, titles and suffixes will not be displayed (Ex: Dr., Mr., Sr., etc.). Job titles will match HRMS. Regular status employees are required to renew photo identification badges every three years. Substitute employees, temporary employees, contracted and volunteers must renew annually.</p>
<p>To report identification badge concerns, please call (702) 799-2846.</p>
