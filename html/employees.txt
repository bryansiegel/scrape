<h3>Employee Tools</h3>
<p>The Employee Tools for Clark County School District give staff easy access to key platforms and services needed to manage their work and employment information. Through this portal, employees can log in to Canvas for course management and professional learning, access Infinite Campus for student information and classroom needs, submit a Change of Address to keep personal records up to date, and complete required Fingerprinting for compliance and security. By bringing these resources together in one location, CCSD helps employees save time and stay connected with the systems that support both teaching and operational excellence.</p>

<h3>Employee Support</h3>
<p>The Employee Support resources for Clark County School District provide staff with the information and services needed to manage their employment and stay connected with district operations. Employees can access the calendar to track important dates and events, review their salary information, and stay informed on policies and procedures that guide their work. The portal also connects staff to ELMS for professional development, Employee Services for HR-related needs, and tools for managing their I.D. Badge. By centralizing these resources, CCSD ensures employees have the support and guidance they need to succeed in their roles.</p>
