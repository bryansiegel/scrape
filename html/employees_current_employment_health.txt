<p>The Clark County School District aims to provide quality, affordable health care coverage to our 40,000
            employees and their dependents. </p>
<p>We negotiate with each employee association to provide either health insurance plans or contribute
            toward health care plans for our employees.  No changes will be made to employee health care plans
            unless they are negotiated with each employee group. Benefits for Clark County School District
            employees are outlined in the <a class="pdflink" href="https://ccsd.net/employees/current/employment/information#agreements" target="_blank">Negotiated Agreements</a>.
        </p>
<p>To give your input on health care coverage, please contact your employee association or <a class="pdflink" href="http://ccsd.net/trustees/contact/all" target="_blank">email the Board of School Trustees</a>.

<p><b>Vera Whole Health</b></p>
<p>All Support and Police Professionals enrolled in the CCSD health insurance have the
                                    added benefit of receiving medical care from <a href="https://patients.verawholehealth.com/" rel="nofollow">Vera Whole
                                        Health</a>. You have more important things to do than sit in a waiting room only
                                    to be rushed through an appointment. That’s why we do healthcare differently,
                                    including flexible appointment options, family care, free health coaching, and
                                    longer visits.</p>
<p>Plus, it’s all part of your CCSD benefits<b><a href="https://ccsd.net/resources/employee-benefits/Its-Free-Really.pdf">—at
                                            no cost to you.</a></b></p>
