<h3>Student Record and Transcript Requests</h3>
<p><strong>Student Record Services</strong><br/>
					702-799-2938</p>
<p>Requests for student records of currently enrolled students should be submitted to the school of attendance.  Requests for student records of former students must be submitted to the Student Record Services department.  Student record requests are submitted online at <a href="http://transcripts.ccsd.net">transcripts.ccsd.net</a> or <a href="https://clarknv.scriborder.com">https://clarknv.scriborder.com</a>. The phone number for Student Record Services is 702-799-2938 if you have any questions.</p>
