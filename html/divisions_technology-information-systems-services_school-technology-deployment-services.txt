<h1>School Technology Deployment Services</h1>
<p>School Technology Deployment Services is responsible for the quality-assured integration of technology in the district's schools. This department performs project management and installation associated with networking over 326 district schools, from server to desktop. This includes equipment installation for both Local Area Network (LAN) and Wide Area Network (WAN) communication and all levels of associated software.</p>
