<h2>
	Civil Infractions</h2>
<p data-end="100" data-start="93">
<strong>NOTICE:</strong></p>
<p data-end="340" data-start="102">
	You have been cited for a civil infraction pursuant to Nevada Revised Statutes (NRS) Chapter 484A. This is a non-criminal offense. You are required to respond to this citation within 90 calendar days from the date the citation was issued or the date printed on your citation.</p>
<p data-end="359" data-start="342">
<strong>Your options are:</strong></p>
<ol data-end="1023" data-start="361">
<li data-end="668" data-start="361">
<p data-end="668" data-start="364">
<u><strong>Pay the civil penalty</strong></u>: By paying the penalty, you admit responsibility for the infraction. Payment can be made online, by mail, or in person</p>
</li>
<li data-end="1023" data-start="670">
<p data-end="1023" data-start="673">
<u><strong>Contest the citation</strong></u>: To dispute the infraction, you must complete and submit a Civil Infraction Response Form and post a bond equal to the total amount of the civil penalty, administrative assessments, and any applicable fees. This must be done in person at the Customer Service Division. Upon submission, a contested hearing date will be scheduled.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p data-end="1084" data-start="1025">
<strong>Failure to respond within the above time period will result in:</strong></p>
<ul data-end="1415" data-start="1086">
<li data-end="1150" data-start="1086">
<p data-end="1150" data-start="1088">
			A default judgment finding you responsible for the infraction.</p>
</li>
<li data-end="1238" data-start="1151">
<p data-end="1238" data-start="1153">
			Imposition of the civil penalty, administrative assessments, and any additional fees.</p>
</li>
<li data-end="1323" data-start="1239">
<p data-end="1323" data-start="1241">
			Referral of the debt to collections, which may include additional collection fees.</p>
</li>
<li data-end="1415" data-start="1324">
<p data-end="1415" data-start="1326">
			Potential suspension of your driver's license by the Nevada Department of Motor Vehicles.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p data-end="1539" data-start="1417">
