<h1>BlendED Initiative</h1>
<p>Blended education, or “BlendED” for short, combines the best aspects of face-to-face learning with the best aspects of online learning, to generate a learning experience for the student that is individualized to meet the needs of every student.</p>
<img alt="" height="246" src="resources/images/teacher-student-tablet.jpg" width="368"/>
<p>The BlendED Initiative promotes best practices of digital learning in CCSD through a collaborative effort of the following branches of the Curriculum and Professional Development Division (CPD):</p>
<ul>
<li>Online and Blended Learning Department</li>
<li>Professional Development Education</li>
<li>Nevada Ready 21 Program</li>
</ul>
