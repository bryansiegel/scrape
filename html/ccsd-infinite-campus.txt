<div>
<p>Infinite Campus is the Clark County School District’s student information system, designed to help students, parents, and staff stay connected and informed. It serves as a central hub for academic records, attendance tracking, grades, schedules, and other important student information. Students can view their assignments, grades, and class schedules, while parents can monitor their child’s progress through an observer account. Teachers and staff use the system to manage classroom data, communicate with families, and streamline administrative tasks, making it an essential tool for supporting student success.</p>
