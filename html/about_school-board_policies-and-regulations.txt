<div class="fsElementContent">
<p>The Clark County School District (CCSD) establishes policies and regulations to guide daily operations, maintain consistency, and ensure compliance with state and federal laws. Policies set the overall direction and expectations approved by the Board of Trustees, while regulations provide detailed procedures for how those policies are carried out across schools and departments. Together, they create a clear framework that promotes accountability, supports student success, and helps staff, families, and the community understand how decisions are made and implemented within the district.</p>

<h2>Notification of proposed changes to CCSD Policies and/or Regulations</h2>
<p>State statute requires the Board of School Trustees adopt a Notice of Intent when it considers the adoption, amendment, or repeal of a policy or regulation. The Notice of Intent provides the public thirteen (13) days notice prior to final action by the board.</p>
<p>Comments by the public are welcome.</p>
<p><strong>No policies or regulations are scheduled at this time.</strong></p>
<p> </p>
<h2>Notice of Intent - Date of Meeting:</h2>
<p>Reflects the date of the board meeting discussion/action will be taken on the Notice of Intent.</p>
<p><strong>No policies or regulations are scheduled at this time.</strong></p>
<p> </p>
<h2>Final Action - Date of Meeting:</h2>
<p>This date will indicate the date of the board meeting when the final discussion/action will occur. This is the last meeting for public comment on the issue.</p>
<p><strong>No policies or regulations are scheduled at this time.</strong></p>
