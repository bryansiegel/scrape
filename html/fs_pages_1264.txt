<h2 class="fsElementTitle"><a aria-controls="fsPanelContent_12093" aria-expanded="false" href="#fs-panel-12093" role="button">Government Relations Department</a></h2>
</header>
<div aria-hidden="true" class="fsElementContent">
<a id="fs-panel-12093"></a>
<div class="fsElement fsContent" data-use-new="true" id="fsEl_12094">
<div class="fsElementContent">
<p>The Government Relations Department builds and sustains close relationships with local, state, and federal officials to increase support for student-centric public policy that supports the unique needs of the District. The Government Relations team represents the District in matters concerning Congress, the Nevada Legislature, the Nevada State Board of Education, the Nevada Department of Education, and local governments. The Department helps:</p>
<ul dir="ltr">
<li>develop legislative priorities; </li>
<li>represent the District at legislative hearings </li>
<li>lobby during the legislative session</li>
<li>meet with legislators during the interim to assist with the education-related concerns of their constituents, and </li>
<li>serve as a District contact on various public policy issues. </li>
</ul>
<p>The office also monitors the District's compliance with legislation during the interim.</p>

<p style="font-weight:bold; font-size: 16px;">The Marketing Department champions the District’s brand by creating content that celebrates the success of CCSD students, teachers, and schools and promotes opportunities for students and employees.</p>
<h3>District Branding</h3>
<ul style="margin:0px !important;">
<li><strong>Brand</strong>
<p style="margin-top:0px !important; padding-top: 0px !important;">Brand consistency is vital to building a solid brand and increasing brand value and trust. Brand guidelines ensure consistency in CCSD's visual and messaging identity, including logos and colors. Adhering to these guidelines builds trust through consistent branding and messaging, enhancing the District's reputation.</p>
<ul>
<li>Writing - <a href="https://graphics.ccsd.net/NewFiles/GAC.2006.Site/PDFs/District_Logo_Standards_Bklt/CCSD
_WritingStandards_2025.pdf" target="_blank">Standards and Style Guide</a></li>
<li><a href="https://graphics.ccsd.net/templates-logos.php" target="_blank">CCSD Logos, Letterhead, Templates</a></li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<h3>Content</h3>
<ul style="margin:0px !important;">
<li><strong>We are CCSD</strong>
<p style="margin-top:0px !important; padding-top: 0px !important;">In January 2023, Superintendent Dr. Jesus F. Jara launched "We are CCSD" during the 2023 State of the Schools Address. This campaign aims to highlight the collaborative efforts of the Southern Nevada community in educating the children of Clark County. At newsroom.ccsd.net, the official blog of Clark County School District, the achievements of CCSD students, teachers, and schools take center stage, celebrating their remarkable successes and contributions to the District's educational journey.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<ul style="margin:0px !important;">
<li><strong>YouTube</strong>
<p style="margin-top:0px !important; padding-top: 0px !important;">YouTube will serve as the primary video library for CCSD and the CCSD brand. This centralized approach to video storage and organization will help maintain a cohesive and comprehensive video library that showcases the diverse offerings and achievements of CCSD.</p>
<ul>
<li><a href="https://www.youtube.com/@ClarkCountySchoolDistrict" target="_blank">https://www.youtube.com/@ClarkCountySchoolDistrict</a></li>
<li><a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=v3twXhUf_Dw" target="_blank">We are Vegas. We Are CCSD</a></li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<h3>External Communications</h3>
<p>Using the web, social media, and email, the Marketing Department will work to provide consistent communication celebrating the successes of CCSD students, teachers, and schools, promoting opportunities for students and employees.</p>
<ul style="margin:0px !important;">
<li><strong>CCSD Website</strong>
<ul style="margin:0px !important;">
<li>Collaborate with Media Relations and Web Design Departments to maintain consistent messaging, District branding, and voice on the main CCSD website (ccsd.net) to ensure a unified online presence and enhance external communication efforts.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><strong>Social Media</strong>
<p style="margin-top:0px !important; padding-top: 0px !important;">Establish and implement best practices on the District’s primary social media channels throughout all departments, which includes crafting a comprehensive social media strategy and ensuring its effective implementation across various teams</p>
<ul>

</li>
<li><strong>Community Newsletter</strong>
<ul style="margin:0px !important;">
<li style="margin-top:0px !important; padding-top: 0px !important;">Connects with the Southern Nevada community through an email newsletter that celebrates the success of CCSD students, teachers, and schools by highlighting achievements; and sharing inspiring stories that fosters pride and unity within the community, strengthen external communications, and promote a positive brand perception</li>
<li><a href="https://clark-county-school-district.read.axioshq.com/signup/community-newsletter" target="_blank">Subscribe to the Community Connection</a></li>
</ul>
</li>

<h3>Internal Communications</h3>
<p>The Marketing Department will ensure clear and consistent communication, which helps employees stay well-informed about developments in the District and know they are valued. The Marketing Department will build trust through improved internal communication, bringing value to the District’s brand.</p>
<ul style="margin:0px !important;">
<li><strong>Employee Email Newsletters</strong>
<p style="margin-top:0px !important; padding-top: 0px !important;">Employee email newsletters are crucial for improving internal communication. Newsletters provide valuable information, such as updates, policies, development opportunities, and success stories. These bi-monthly newsletters keep employees well-informed and supported, fostering engagement and offering professional growth opportunities.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h3>Marketing Support and Resources</h3>
<p>The Marketing Department will establish a centralized marketing support system that provides resources, guidance, and best practices for schools and departments to develop effective marketing strategies. This support system may include workshops, training sessions, or access to marketing professionals who can assist with the creation of promotional materials and campaigns. By equipping other entities within CCSD with the necessary tools and knowledge, the District can collectively elevate its brand through collaborative marketing efforts.</p>
<h1 style="padding-top:25px;">Web Design Department</h1>
