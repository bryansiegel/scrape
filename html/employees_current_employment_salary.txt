<h2>Employment and Payroll Verification</h2>

<p><strong>Forms: Public Service Loan Forgiveness, DoDEA Form 5013, and Heartland/Perkins Deferment</strong><br>
<p>District employees may obtain employment and payroll verification through Experian Verify. This service offers secure, confidential internet access and telephone support Mon-Fri 9am – 7pm (ET).</p>
<p>To access <strong>Employment Verification Information</strong>, verifiers will need to register and submit their requests online at <a href="https://www.experian.com/employer-services/products/verification-fulfillment/experian-verify" target="_blank">www.experianverify.com</a>. They will need the following information:</p>
<ol>
<li>Your full Social Security Number</li>
<li>Your First and Last Name</li>
<li>Name of Employer (Clark County School District)</li>
<li>A signed authorization form (for income verification)</li>
</ol>
<p>No company code or salary pin required. If your verifier has questions or encounter issues, they can contact customer support by calling (404) 382-5400, option 2.</p>
<p>Do you have a <strong>Public Service Loan Forgiveness</strong> form? You can submit your request to Experian Verify by fax at (404) 829-1336 or by email to <a href="https://voie.experian.com/app/ee/eeregister.aspx" target="_blank">https://voie.experian.com/app/ee/eeregister.aspx</a>. Select “create an employee account” and enter the following:</p>
<ol>
<li>Access code: 4E13D10A</li>
<li>Full Social Security Number</li>
<li>Your Full Name</li>
<li>Date of Birth</li>
<li>Email Address</li>
</ol>
<p>Login with your Credentials by following prompts: A password setup link will be sent to your email address. Click the link to create a password and activate your account. Once logged in select the “Letters” on homepage or sidebar menu. Select “PSLF Letter”. A letter with submittal instructions and completed ECF will be generated.<p>
<p>Do you have a <strong>Social Service Request</strong> from a government agency? Please submit your request to Experian Verify by fax at (404) 829-1336 or by email to <a href="mailto:verify@experian.com">verify@experian.com</a>. You can also have your caseworker send the request directly to this fax or email. Be sure to include the following information on the form:</p>
<ol>
<li>Your Full Name</li>
<li>The last 4 of your Social Security Number</li>
<li>A return fax number for the social security agency</li>
</ol>
<p>Requests will be processed within one (1) business day. If you have any questions, you can contact Experian Verify’s social service department at (404) 382-5400, option 4.</p>
					
					The <strong>Experian Verify Employee Portal</strong> provides secure employment and/or income information to our credentialed verifiers (mortgage companies, lenders, pre-employment screeners, etc.) on behalf of your employer. You may view your report and control access to your information using the Experian Verify employee portal. Visit <a href="https://www.experian.com/employer-services/products/verification-fulfillment/experian-verify" target="_blank">www.experianverify.com</a> and choose “I am an Employee”. Enter the following:
						<ol>
<li>Access code: 4E13D10A</li>
<li>Full Social Security Number</li>
<li>Your Full Name</li>
<li>Date of Birth</li>
<li>Email Address</li>
</ol>
<p>Click Create an Employee Account. A password setup link will be sent to your email address. Click the link to create a password and activate your account. Lastly log in with your credentials. </p>
<p><strong>Forms: Verification of Teaching Experience, Fedloan Teach Grant Certification, and Teacher Loan Forgiveness, click <a href="https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSdrlyZF6MQ5VxpcYBbFq_CUNarAjmd2RPb2B1U3LZj1OFk0jA/viewform" target="_blank">here</a><!-- 						click <a href="https://docs.google.com/forms/d/1f6qY67dFUzwNa9KlXM-__tuVBrq798IpzJqE-KaUsQY/viewform" target="_blank">here</a>  -->for CCSD to complete the request.
</strong></p>
