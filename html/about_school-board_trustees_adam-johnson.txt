<div style="margin-left: 10px;">
<p>Adam Johnson is an educator, executive leader and community advocate who spent 6 years as the founding Executive Director at Democracy Prep at the Agassi Campus (DPAC), college prep K-12 school serving more than 1,200 children in West Las Vegas. During his tenure at DPAC, Adam helped the school achieve a 5-star rating from the NV Department of Education. He currently serves as a Senior Director for College Board's Western Region.</p>
<p>Adam earned his MBA from George Washington University in Washington, D.C. and his BA in Accounting and Economics at St. Michael’s College in Burlington, Vermont.</p>
<p>Today, he resides in Las Vegas with his wife, 16-year old son and 12-year old daughter.</p>
