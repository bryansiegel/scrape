<h1 align="left">
	Complaints</h1>
<p>
	The Clark County School District Police Department regards the investigation of all complaints as important to its overall mission. If you feel that you have a complaint against an employee of the Clark County School District Police Department, please contact the Department directly. The Internal Affairs Bureau will make every effort to ensure that the information provided to us is held in the strictest confidence.</p>
<p>
	CCSDPD will accept any form of complaint. Each complaint is taken very seriously and will always be fairly and impartially investigated. It is impossible to put a time limit on any investigation, as the seriousness of the allegations vary, but be assured that every effort will be made to update specific complainants about specific cases.</p>
<p>
<strong>As a reminder, this form is for complaints against school district police officers.  If you have a concern regarding a school district employee, please contact the appropriate site administrator.</strong></p>
<p>
	If you have a TRAFFIC COMPLAINT, please send those to: <a href="mailto:traffic@nv.ccsd.net?subject=Traffic%20Complaint%20&amp;body=What%20school%20is%20this%20happening%20at%3F%0A%0AWhat%20cross-streets%20or%20area%20of%20the%20school%20is%20it%20happening%3F%0A%0AWhen%20is%20it%20happening%3F%20%20AM%20or%20PM%3F%20%20%0A%0AWhat%20is%20the%20violation%20that%20you%20are%20seeing%3F%0A%0AAny%20other%20information%20you%20would%20like%20to%20provide%3F">traffic@nv.ccsd.net</a></p>
<p>
<strong>The Internal Affairs Bureau can be reached anytime between the hours of 8:00 A.M. and 4:00 P.M., Monday through Friday at (702) 799-7830 or fax your complaint to (702) </strong></p>
