<h2 style="font-size:25px !important;">Your support and investment are vital</h2>
<p>The Clark County School District (CCSD) is committed to ensuring every student is prepared for a bright future. As the developer of Southern Nevada's future workforce and talent, CCSD’s success is our community’s success.</p>
<p>We are building the Destination District—but we cannot do it alone. Your support and investment is vital.</p>
<p>We invite businesses, organizations, and community members to partner with us. Whether you can offer expertise, resources, or volunteer time, there is a role for you in shaping our students' future. Join us in creating the Destination District!</p>
