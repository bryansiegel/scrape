<h2 class="fsElementTitle"><a aria-controls="fsPanelContent_15806" aria-expanded="false" href="#fs-panel-15806" role="button">Employment and Payroll Verification</a></h2>
<div class="fsElementHeaderContent">
<div><a id="employment-verification"></a></div>
</div>
</header>
<div aria-hidden="true" class="fsElementContent">
<a id="fs-panel-15806"></a>
<div class="fsElement fsContent" data-use-new="true" id="fsEl_15807">
<div class="fsElementContent">
<p><strong>Forms: Public Service Loan Forgiveness, DoDEA Form 5013, and Heartland/Perkins Deferment</strong></p>
<p>District employees may obtain employment and payroll verification through Experian Verify. This service offers secure, confidential internet access and telephone support Mon-Fri 9am – 7pm (ET).</p>
<p>To access <strong>Employment Verification Information</strong>, verifiers will need to register and submit their requests online at <a href="https://www.experian.com/employer-services/products/verification-fulfillment/experian-verify" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">www.experianverify.com</a>. They will need the following information:</p>
<ol>
<li>Your full Social Security Number</li>
