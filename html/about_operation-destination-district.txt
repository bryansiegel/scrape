<h3 class="number-title">Mile Marker <span class="badge-red">1</span></h3>
<h4>Spring and Summer, 2025</h4>
<p>100-Day Plan focused on climate and culture completed and presented to community</p>
<p>During Superintendent Ebert’s first 100 days in the role, she and the CCSD leadership team implemented a 100-Day Plan to rebuild climate and culture. This was the first step to paving the way for the long-term efforts ahead.</p>
<p><strong>CCSD also celebrated key “wins” that are building momentum, including:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Record staffing levels - 98% of classrooms staffed with a licensed teacher</li>
<li>Improved graduation rate (86.6% for the 2024-2025 school year)</li>
<li>Added 500 Pre-K seats during the 2025-2026 school year</li>
<li>136 CCSD schools improved their star ratings</li>
<li>Improved literacy rates in third grade</li>
<li>Improved middle school math proficiency</li>
<li>Significant growth in dual enrollment, the number of students with AP scores 3 or higher, and the percentage of students passing workplace readiness skills assessments</li>
</ul>

<h3 class="number-title">Mile Marker <span class="badge-orange">2</span></h3>
<h4>Fall, 2025 and Winter, 2026</h4>
<ul>
<li>Academic focus on the “Core Four”: Pre-Kindergarten, Early Literacy, Middle School Math, and Workforce and College Readiness. <strong>Cour Four Presentation <a href="https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1e2XSCLdk50wmPrvWsDa30_Pfmtnw50ukKT7AycY3Xy8/edit?slide=id.g3b335bacd20_0_24#slide=id.g3b335bacd20_0_24" target="_blank">English </a>(<a contenteditable="false" data-ved="2ahUKEwiCj9SVrM-TAxX2JUQIHYH0PAAQFnoECDAQAQ" href="https://ccsd.community.diligentoneplatform.com/document/cd78780e-9c50-4a3e-ac4b-283de378add4/" target="_blank">Español</a>)</strong></li>
<li>Robust community input to co-design strategic plan and Facility Master Plan</li>
</ul>
<p>CCSD is preparing a new strategic plan to shape our future. This plan will be built on the foundation of unprecedented community feedback, including nearly 50,000 survey responses from our community this fall. We’re also conducting stakeholder interviews with leaders throughout our community and “Community Dream Sessions” throughout Southern Nevada.</p>
