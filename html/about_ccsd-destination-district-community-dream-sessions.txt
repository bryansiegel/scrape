<h2>We’re listening!</h2>
<p>Over the last five months, our community has been speaking up. The What We Heard Report captures their voices and ideas for how we can make CCSD the Destination District—where every student, employee, and family chooses to belong because they believe in what’s possible.</p>
<p><a class="button-1" data-file-name="WhatWeHeard_Singles_Final_129.pdf" data-resource-uuid="30b068cb-868e-4be7-b2e8-1e30f832f43a" href="/fs/resource-manager/view/30b068cb-868e-4be7-b2e8-1e30f832f43a" target="_blank">Full Report</a> <a class="button-1" data-file-name="WhatWeHeard_Report_Spanish_Singles.pdf" data-resource-uuid="39101f3d-3519-4177-a8fc-4f36938863d5" href="/fs/resource-manager/view/39101f3d-3519-4177-a8fc-4f36938863d5" target="_blank">Full Report - Español</a></p>
<p>CCSD needs your help as we build a strategic plan that will serve as our blueprint to becoming a Destination District!</p>
<p>We are hosting a series of in-person and virtual Community Dream Sessions where students, families, and employees can help design what the Destination District looks and feels like.</p>
<p>How to Participate</p>
<p>Bring your perspective to an upcoming Community Dream Session:</p>
