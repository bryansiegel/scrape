<h1>Get Involved</h1>
<p><img alt="get-involved" class="portrait float-right" height="214" src="images/get-involved.jpg" width="300">
                    Each person holds the power to make a positive impact on Clark County's 316,000 students.</img></p>
<p>By getting involved, you can help build our workforce and improve our economy. You can also make the difference in the life of a child!</p>
<p>First, take the Pledge of Achievement to learn more about the District's strategic vision and find out how you can help. </p>
<p>Help a student to graduate by mentoring him or her. We need community mentors who are willing to give a student one hour a week.</p>
<p>Our School-Community Partnership program teams with schools, businesses, community organizations and parents to <a href="https://engage.ccsd.net/partnershipopportunities/">make a difference</a> in the lives of students.</p>
<p>Our <a href="/schools/support-a-school">Support a School</a> program allows schools to list items they need that go beyond school supplies such as paper, pencils, backpacks and tissues.</p>
<p>The Clark County READS program asks community members to read with two students one hour a week for 12 weeks to support reading skills that are fundamental in the classroom and beyond. The program will provide an orientation, we just need your dedication and support!</p>
<p>Teachers thrive with creative resources, and you can help in various ways through the Teacher EXCHANGE™</p>
<p>Your voice counts. <a href="/trustees">Attend a meeting of the CCSD Board of Trustees</a>, ask about serving on a board committee or participate in your trustee's parent meetings.</p>
