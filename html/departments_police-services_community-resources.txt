<h3 data-end="254" data-start="203" style="text-align: justify;">
<strong data-end="254" data-start="203">Juvenile-Related Incidents and Community Safety</strong></h3>
<p data-end="614" data-start="256" style="text-align: justify;">
	If you are a neighboring business or property owner experiencing disturbances or incidents involving juveniles or students, please notify both your local law enforcement agency and the Clark County School District Police Dispatch. Coordinated communication with local police agencies helps us better serve the community and address concerns more effectively.</p>
<h3 data-end="641" data-start="616" style="text-align: justify;">
<strong data-end="641" data-start="616">Community Partnership</strong></h3>
<p data-end="993" data-start="643" style="text-align: justify;">
	Open communication and information sharing are key to maintaining a safe and supportive environment. If you witness or are aware of student-related incidents, do not hesitate to contact School Police or the on-site school administrator. Strong partnerships between schools, law enforcement, and the community are essential to long-term public safety.</p>
