<ul>
<li data-end="422" data-start="239">
<p data-end="422" data-start="242" style="text-align: justify;">
<strong data-end="266" data-start="242">Stay Alert and Aware</strong><br data-end="269" data-start="266">
			Always be aware of your surroundings. Report suspicious behavior, unattended bags, or unknown individuals to school staff or <a href="tel:7027995411">law enforcement immediately.</a>  <span style="color: #ff0000;"><strong>FOR EMERGENCIES, CALL 911!</strong></span></br></p>
</li>
<li data-end="588" data-start="424" style="text-align: justify;">
<p data-end="588" data-start="427">
<strong data-end="456" data-start="427">Lock It, Hide It, Keep It</strong><br data-end="459" data-start="456">
			Lock your car and keep valuables out of sight. Don’t leave backpacks, electronics, or personal items <em>unattended or visible</em> inside your vehicle.</br></p>
</li>
<li data-end="756" data-start="590" style="text-align: justify;">
<p data-end="756" data-start="593">
<strong data-end="631" data-start="593">Practice Safe Drop-Off and Pick-Up</strong><br data-end="634" data-start="631"/>
			Obey school zone speed limits and traffic signs. Use only designated drop-off/pick-up areas and avoid blocking travel lanes, crosswalks, or driveways.</p>
</li>
<li data-end="910" data-start="758" style="text-align: justify;">
<p data-end="910" data-start="761">
<strong data-end="791" data-start="761">Secure Personal Belongings</strong><br data-end="794" data-start="791"/>
			Remind students to lock their lockers and secure items. Staff should lock classrooms and workspaces when unattended.</p>
</li>
<li data-end="1080" data-start="912" style="text-align: justify;">
<p data-end="1080" data-start="915">
<strong data-end="932" data-start="915">Say Something</strong><br data-end="935" data-start="932"/>
			If you see or hear something concerning — online or in person — <strong><a href="https://www.p3campus.com/tipform.aspx?ID=5004">report it</a></strong> immediately. Anonymous tip lines and school staff are always available.</p>
</li>
<li data-end="1238" data-start="1082" style="text-align: justify;">
<p data-end="1238" data-start="1085">
<strong data-end="1110" data-start="1085">Be Social Media Smart</strong><br data-end="1113" data-start="1110"/>
			Think before you post. Never share personal information or location details publicly. Report cyberbullying or online threats.</p>
</li>
<li data-end="1411" data-start="1240" style="text-align: justify;">
<p data-end="1411" data-start="1243">
<strong data-end="1272" data-start="1243">Know Emergency Procedures</strong><br data-end="1275" data-start="1272"/>
			Students and staff should know the school’s safety drills and emergency plans. Parents should also stay informed about school protocols.</p>
</li>
<li data-end="1566" data-start="1413" style="text-align: justify;">
<p data-end="1566" data-start="1416">
<strong data-end="1444" data-start="1416">Keep School Grounds Safe</strong><br data-end="1447" data-start="1444"/>
			Report broken locks, lights, or gates to maintenance or school personnel. Small fixes can prevent bigger safety issues.</p>
</li>
<li data-end="1718" data-start="1568" style="text-align: justify;">
<p data-end="1718" data-start="1571">
<strong data-end="1603" data-start="1571">Build Positive Relationships</strong><br data-end="1606" data-start="1603"/>
			A connected community is a safer one. Get to know your assigned Police Officers, teachers, administrators and staff members.</p>
</li>
<li data-end="1910" data-start="1720">
<p data-end="1910" data-start="1724" style="text-align: justify;">
<strong data-end="1761" data-start="1724">Encourage Safe Walking and Biking</strong><br data-end="1764" data-start="1761"/>
			Use sidewalks, crosswalks, and bike lanes. Avoid distractions like phones or headphones when crossing streets. Follow crossing guard instructions.</p>
</li>
</ul>
