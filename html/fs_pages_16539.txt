<div class="fsElementContent">
<p>The Family and Medical Leave Act (FMLA) allows eligible employees to take up to 12 weeks of job-protected leave for qualifying family and medical reasons. In certain cases, employees may take up to 26 weeks of leave to care for a covered servicemember who has a serious injury or illness sustained while on active duty. To utilize paid leave, the leave reason must meet the criteria outlined in your specific negotiated agreement.</p>

<h2>Leave of Absence (LOA)</h2>
<p>All Clark County School District (CCSD) employees must meet specific requirements to be granted a leave of absence. The typical duration for a leave of absence is one year, unless otherwise specified in the relevant regulations.</p>
