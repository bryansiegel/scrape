<h3>Questions About Medicare?</h3>
<p>Are you nearing age 65 and have questions about your Medicare options? You can talk or live chat with a real person, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week (except some federal holidays).</p>
<p><strong>1-800-MEDICARE (1-800-633-4227)</strong><br/>
<a href="https://www.medicare.gov/talk-to-someone" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">Contact Medicare</a></p>
<p>If you or a dependent are enrolling in Medicare Part B, the Benefits Department completes the CMS-L564 (CMS-R-297) on your behalf. Section B is the employer portion of this form that confirms the dates of enrollment in the CCSD medical plan. Please call our team to request this form for you or your dependent. We are available Monday - Friday, 7:30am to 5:00pm.</p>

<h3>403(b) and 457(b) Savings Plans</h3>
<p>CCSD continually strives to offer and educate staff about our unique and advantageous benefits. One such benefit is our elective <strong>Tax Savings Retirement Savings Plan(s) </strong>that employees can utilize to help provide financial security at retirement. For guides and annual benefit limits, please visit the link below. TSA Consulting Group, Inc. (TSACG) is the compliance administrator for our plan(s).</p>
<p><a href="http://www.tsacg.com/individual/plan-sponsor/nevada/clark-county-school-district/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">Tax Savings Retirement Plans</a><br/>
<a data-file-name="Approved-403b-457b-Vendors-02012024.pdf" data-resource-uuid="09dbd90c-bd81-4d61-a1fc-135aa99cbe35" href="/fs/resource-manager/view/09dbd90c-bd81-4d61-a1fc-135aa99cbe35" target="_blank">Approved 403(b) and 457(b) Vendors (PDF)</a> (updated 2/01/2024)</p>
<p>To further encourage participation in our voluntary retirement plans, CCSD now offers an easy and quick 403(b) enrollment option called <strong>QuickENROLL.</strong> This new option helps employees rapidly enroll in a 403(b) retirement savings account without having to conduct initial research or meet with an Investment Provider representative.</p>
<p><a href="https://www.myquickenroll.com/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">www.myquickenroll.com</a></p>
<p>In addition, the <a href="https://usrbpfinancialwellness.com/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">Financial Wellness</a> page covers areas such as managing personal finances, college funding options, and how to navigate Social Security when the time comes.
