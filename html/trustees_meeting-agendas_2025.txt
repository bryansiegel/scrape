<h1>School Board Meetings</h1>
<p> <strong>Livestream Meetings</strong><br/>
		All meetings of the Board of Trustees, including committees of the Board, are livestreamed the day of the meeting by one of the following ways.</p>
<p><strong>District Website</strong><br/>
		On the day of the meeting a link to the meeting can be found on the District’s home webpage <a href="https://ccsd.net/">here</a>. Look for the link in the middle of the home page. Click on the Live Streaming icon to begin viewing. Please call 702-799-2988, for assistance accessing the meeting.</p>
<p><strong>YouTube</strong><br/>
		Link to the District’s YouTube channel <a href="https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCb8dUIsat7U7lTjXYPFs_Ww" target="_blank">here</a>.</p>
<p><strong>Eduvision</strong><br/>
		Link to Eduvision <a href="https://ccsd.eduvision.tv/LiveEvents" target="_blank">here</a>.</p>
<p><strong>Video Replays</strong><br/>
		All meetings of the Board of Trustees, including committees of the Board, are recorded and generally available the next business day for viewing by clicking on the Watch link located in the Video column below.</p>
<p><strong>BoardDocs</strong><br/>
	Access meeting agendas and reference material in BoardDocs by linking <a href="https://go.boarddocs.com/nv/ccsdlv/Board.nsf/Public" target="_blank">here</a> or by clicking on the icon located in the View BoardDocs column.</p>
