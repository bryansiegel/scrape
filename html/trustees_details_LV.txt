<h2 style="margin: 0 0 5px;">Member</h2>
<div class="trustee-bio" style="background-color: #fbfbfb; border: 1px solid #e3e3e3;">
<p>Adam Johnson is an educator, executive leader and community advocate who spent 6 years as the founding Executive Director at Democracy Prep at the Agassi Campus (DPAC),
college prep K-12 school serving more than 1,200 children in West Las Vegas. During his tenure at DPAC, Adam helped the school achieve a 5-star rating from the NV Department of
Education. He currently serves as a Senior Director for College Board's Western Region.

 
	
	
	
	 
	
</p>
