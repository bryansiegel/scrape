<h2 data-end="153" data-start="54">
	Civil vs. Criminal Traffic Citations </h2>
<h3>
	What is the difference?</h3>
<p data-end="204" data-start="179">
<u><strong>Civil Traffic Citation:</strong></u></p>
<ul data-end="419" data-start="205">
<li data-end="229" data-start="205">
<p data-end="229" data-start="207">
			Non-criminal offense</p>
</li>
<li data-end="256" data-start="230">
<p data-end="256" data-start="232">
			No jail time or arrest</p>
</li>
<li data-end="279" data-start="257">
<p data-end="279" data-start="259">
			No criminal record</p>
</li>
<li data-end="313" data-start="280">
<p data-end="313" data-start="282">
			Handled like a parking ticket</p>
</li>
<li data-end="350" data-start="314">
<p data-end="350" data-start="316">
			Cannot result in a bench warrant</p>
</li>
<li data-end="419" data-start="351">
<p data-end="419" data-start="353">
			Examples: minor speeding, failure to yield, equipment violations</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p data-end="449" data-start="421">
<u><strong>Criminal Traffic Citation:</strong></u></p>
<ul data-end="720" data-start="450">
<li data-end="481" data-start="450">
<p data-end="481" data-start="452">
			Misdemeanor criminal charge</p>
</li>
<li data-end="514" data-start="482">
<p data-end="514" data-start="484">
			Can result in arrest or jail</p>
</li>
<li data-end="547" data-start="515">
<p data-end="547" data-start="517">
			Goes on your criminal record</p>
</li>
<li data-end="591" data-start="548">
<p data-end="591" data-start="550">
			Requires court appearance in many cases</p>
</li>
<li data-end="645" data-start="592">
<p data-end="645" data-start="594">
			Can lead to a bench warrant if you fail to appear</p>
</li>
<li data-end="720" data-start="646">
<p data-end="720" data-start="648">
			Examples: DUI, reckless driving, driving without license, no insurance</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p data-end="761" data-start="722">
<u><strong>Civil Traffic Citations (Post-AB116):</strong></u></p>
<ul data-end="1253" data-start="762">
<li data-end="846" data-start="762">
<p data-end="846" data-start="764">
			Handled as civil infractions under Nevada law (AB116, effective January 1, 2023)</p>
</li>
<li data-end="920" data-start="847">
<p data-end="920" data-start="849">
			Issued for low-level traffic offenses (non-injury, non-crash-related)</p>
</li>
<li data-end="996" data-start="921">
<p data-end="996" data-start="923">
			You’ll get a due date and options to pay the fine or contest the ticket</p>
</li>
<li data-end="1036" data-start="997">
<p data-end="1036" data-start="999">
			No arrest, jail, or criminal record</p>
</li>
<li data-end="1121" data-start="1037">
<p data-end="1121" data-start="1039">
			Failure to pay may still result in license suspension, late fees, or collections</p>
</li>
<li data-end="1253" data-start="1122">
<p data-end="1253" data-start="1124">
			Handled by: Las Vegas Justice Court (for unincorporated areas) or municipal courts in Las Vegas, Henderson, North Las Vegas, etc.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p data-end="1284" data-start="1255">
<u><strong>Criminal Traffic Citations:</strong></u></p>
<ul data-end="1696" data-start="1285">
<li data-end="1329" data-start="1285">
<p data-end="1329" data-start="1287">
			Considered misdemeanors under Nevada law</p>
</li>
<li data-end="1373" data-start="1330">
<p data-end="1373" data-start="1332">
			May involve mandatory court appearances</p>
</li>
<li data-end="1436" data-start="1374">
<p data-end="1436" data-start="1376">
			Failure to appear can result in a bench warrant and arrest</p>
</li>
<li data-end="1495" data-start="1437">
<p data-end="1495" data-start="1439">
			May carry jail time, fines, and points on your license</p>
</li>
<li data-end="1533" data-start="1496">
<p data-end="1533" data-start="1498">
			You have the right to an attorney</p>
</li>
<li data-end="1696" data-start="1534">
<p data-end="1696" data-start="1536">
			Examples include: DUI, reckless driving, driving without a valid license, driving without insurance or registration, hit and run, driving on a suspended license.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p data-end="1731" data-start="1698">
<u><strong>How to Tell Which One You Have:</strong></u></p>
<ul data-end="1998" data-start="1732">
<li data-end="1856" data-start="1732">
<p data-end="1856" data-start="1734">
			Look at your citation. It will say either "CIVIL" or "CRIMINAL" in the top right hand corner of your citation.</p>
</li>
<li data-end="1954" data-start="1857">
<p data-end="1954" data-start="1859">
			Check the court website and enter your citation information.</p>
</li>
<li data-end="1998" data-start="1955">
<p data-end="1998" data-start="1957">
			Call the court directly if you’re unsure.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p data-end="2015" data-start="2000">
<u><strong>Helpful Tips:</strong></u></p>
<ul data-end="2325" data-start="2016">
<li data-end="2089" data-start="2016">
<p data-end="2089" data-start="2018">
			Don’t ignore a citation. Civil or criminal, consequences still apply.</p>
</li>
<li data-end="2138" data-start="2090">
<p data-end="2138" data-start="2092">
			Civil citations can often be handled online.</p>
</li>
<li data-end="2233" data-start="2139">
<p data-end="2233" data-start="2141">
			Criminal citations may affect job applications, background checks, and driving privileges.</p>
</li>
<li data-end="2325" data-start="2234">
<p data-end="2325" data-start="2236">
			You can always consult a traffic attorney if you're unsure of your rights or next steps.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>
	 </p>
