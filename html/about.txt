<div>
<p>Established in 1956, the<strong> Clark County School District (CCSD)</strong> is the number one choice for families and students. As the nation’s <strong>fifth-largest school district</strong>, we educate <strong>280,000 students</strong> – offering a variety of nationally recognized programs, including Magnet Schools, Career and Technical Academies, and Advanced Placement programs. CCSD educates 64 percent of the students in Nevada and works closely with community partners and business leaders to educate students to compete in a global economy.</p>

<h3>Operation: Destination District</h3>
<p>CCSD is at a pivotal moment for students, families, staff members, and our entire community as we work to transform our schools to meet the needs of today’s students and workforce.</p>
<p>Superintendent Ebert’s 100-Day plan made strides in improving CCSD’s climate and culture. We’re building on increased student achievement with our focus on the “Core Four” academic priorities.</p>
<h5>Up next</h5>
<p>We are co-designing a strategic plan to set goals and allocate resources, a Facilities Master Plan to ensure every student has access to an up-to-date school with the technology and programming they need to succeed, and an efficiency study to ensure every taxpayer dollar is maximized.</p>

<h3>CCSD Board of Trustees</h3>
<p>The Clark County Board of School Trustees is a dedicated group of community leaders who are elected and/or appointed to overlapping four-year terms and represent a specific geographic region of Clark County. Although each trustee represents a different region, they are dedicated to ensuring the success of every student in the district through clear, concise direction to the superintendent. The trustees bring a wealth of experience and concern for children to their position.</p>

<h2>Superintendent</h2>
<h3>Jhone Ebert</h3>
<p>Jhone Ebert brings over three decades of experience, passion, and proven leadership to her Superintendent of the Clark County School District (CCSD) role. A longtime Southern Nevada resident and former CCSD educator, Superintendent Ebert has dedicated her career to ensuring that every student—no matter their background or zip code—has the opportunity to succeed in school and life.</p>

<h2 class="fsElementTitle"><a aria-controls="fsPanelContent_136817" aria-expanded="false" href="#fs-panel-136817" role="button">A Diverse and Inclusive Community</a></h2>

<div aria-hidden="true" class="fsElementContent">

<div class="fsElement fsContent" data-use-new="true" id="fsEl_136818">
<div class="fsElementContent">
<p>Our student body reflects the rich cultural diversity of Southern Nevada, representing <strong>133 countries</strong> and speaking <strong>114 languages</strong>. The district’s demographics include:</p>
<ul>
<li>48.3% Hispanic/Latino</li>
<li>20.0% White/Caucasian</li>
<li>15.9% Black/African American</li>
<li>6.1% Multi-racial</li>
<li>1.6% Asian</li>
<li>0.3% Hawaiian/Pacific Islander</li>
<li>0.3% American Indian/Alaska Native</li>
</ul>

<ul>

<li><strong>8 Career &amp; Technical Academies</strong></li>
