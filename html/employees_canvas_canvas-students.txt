<h2>Canvas LMS Mobile Apps</h2>
<div style="float: left;padding-right: 7px;margin-top: 20px;"><img alt="Canvas LMS Mobile App" src="/employees/canvas/images/CanvasApp.png" width="100px"/></div><p>Instructure has produced a Canvas app available for iOS or Android. Search for “Instructure” to find the one available for your device.</p>
