	School Accounting</h1>
<p>
	The School Accounting Department of the Clark County School District is here to meet the external needs of the community as well as the internal needs of the students and staff of CCSD.</p>
<p>

<h3>
	Facility Usage</h3>
<p>
	The Clark County School District's Facility Usage Department is available to meet the rental needs of the community. We are thrilled to offer our facilities for your next event. As you peruse this web page you will find information on the facility rental process. The Facility Usage Department currently schedules community events at 363 schools throughout the Clark County.</p>
