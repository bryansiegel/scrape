<p>The Clark County School District closely monitors enrollment trends, student demographics, and residential growth to ensure every school is prepared to meet the needs of its community. By carefully managing classroom space and planning for future development, CCSD works to maintain a stable and supportive learning environment for more than 280,000 students across 374 schools. To find your school(s), click on Zoning Search below. Questions may be directed to the <a href="https://sites.google.com/nv.ccsd.net/dzgwebsite/dzg" target="_blank">Demographics and Zoning Website</a>.</p>
