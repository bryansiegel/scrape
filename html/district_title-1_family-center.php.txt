<p>The Title I Family Engagement Centers program is a rapidly growing family engagement and parent education initiative in the Clark County School District, promoting student achievement through a wide variety of parent education and student support services.  Operating through site-based centers as well as an innovative outreach program, our initiative offers customized needs assessments to each of the Title I schools and works with them to create a site-specific action plan for family engagement.</p>
<h2>Mission</h2>
<p>Family Centers’ mission is to optimize the power of Family Engagement by providing and promoting educational opportunities to families that promote increased achievement for every student.</p>
<ul>Families are IMPORTANT.
				<ul style="list-style: disc;">
<li>Research indicates that Family engagement is one of the strongest indicators of a child’s academic success.</li>
<li>Families as powerful advocates effectively impact children’s</li>
<ul>
<li>brain development</li>
<li>language acquisition</li>
<li>self-regulation</li>
<li>graduation rates</li>
<li>pursuit of post-secondary education</li>
</ul>
</ul>
</ul>
