<h2 class="h2-style page-title mb-2">School Zone Enforcement (CCSDPD)</h2>
<div class="tabs">
<div class="faq-item">
<div class="tab-content active">
<p>The CCSDPD Traffic Bureau is focused on ensuring the safety of students, staff, and the community through proactive traffic enforcement, school zone safety operations, and education initiatives. The bureau helps to enforce the following laws in school zones:
		                  <ul>
<li>Reduced speed limits when zone signs are flashing </li>
<ul>
<li>School zones are active 30 minutes before school starts and 30 minutes after school ends </li>
<li>Many schools do not have flashing lights, and are considered ACTIVE when 2 or more students are present within that timeframe.</li>
</ul>
<li>No U-turns in active school zones </li>
<li>No stopping in bus loading zones </li>
<li>No parking in designated school zone areas during enforcement hours</li>
<li>Traffic violations on school district property</li>
<li>Moving and parking violations in active school zones </li>
<li>Traffic violations at active bus stops (when buses are loading/unloading) </li>
<li>School-related traffic incidents involving students or staff</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<br/>
<p align="center">To report traffic concerns in school zones:<br/>
<strong>CCSDPD Dispatch</strong>: <a href="tel:(702)-799-5411" style="font-weight:bold;">702-799-5411</a><br/>Available 24/7 for urgent traffic safety issues<br/>
<a class="button-blue-standard" href="mailto:traffic@nv.ccsd.net" style="text-transform: uppercase;" target="_blank">report a school zone traffic concern</a></p>
</p></div>
</div>
