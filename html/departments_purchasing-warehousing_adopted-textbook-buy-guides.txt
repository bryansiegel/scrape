<h1>
	Adopted Textbook Buy Guides</h1>
<p>
	The Buy Guides are for Adopted Textbooks that can be ordered through the SAP system. See <a href="/resources/purchasing-warehousing/adopted-textbook-catalog-help-guide-181220.pdf">Adopted Textbooks Catalog Help Guide</a>. Scroll through the list or use your browser’s find function (Ctrl+f) to locate textbooks by subject or course number. If you have any questions, please contact <a href="http://www.ccsd.net/departments/purchasing-warehousing">Purchasing &amp; Warehousing</a> (702) 799-5225.</p>
<p>
<strong>Note</strong>: In a buy guide, textbook material is listed in bold text. Supplementary material is in regular text. The word “Gratis” in a Title indicates additional supplements are provided with the textbook. The words “No Gratis” in a Title indicate additional supplements are not provided with the textbook.</p>
