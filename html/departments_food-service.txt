<h2>
	Food Service</h2>
<p>
	The Clark County School District participates in the Community Eligibility Provision of the federal Healthy, Hunger-Free Kids Act district wide and will provide free school meals to all students during the 2025-2026 school year.<br>
	 </br></p>
<p>
	The Clark County School District Food Service Department is committed to acknowledging parent inquiries within a 48 hour period. A Food Service Supervisor or another department employee will respond to your concern and resolve the matter as soon as possible. Food Service would like to ensure that your child receives tasty, nutritious meals and you receive the assistance you require in a timely manner. Please call 799-8123, Ext. 5001 if you have any questions or concerns.<br>
	 </br></p>
