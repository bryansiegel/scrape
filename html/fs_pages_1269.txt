<header>
<h2 class="fsElementTitle"><a aria-controls="fsPanelContent_12108" aria-expanded="false" href="#fs-panel-12108" role="button">Academic Unit</a></h2>
</header>
<div aria-hidden="true" class="fsElementContent">
<a id="fs-panel-12108"></a>
<div class="fsElement fsContent" data-use-new="true" id="fsEl_12109">
<div class="fsElementContent">
			The Academic Unit within the Clark County School District (CCSD) is responsible for overseeing and supporting the district’s instructional programs and academic initiatives. Its primary focus is to ensure all students receive a high-quality education aligned with state standards and district goals. The unit provides leadership in curriculum development, instructional strategies, assessment, and professional learning to drive student achievement across all grade levels. By working closely with schools, educators, and other departments, the Academic Unit plays a vital role in advancing educational equity, fostering innovation, and promoting continuous improvement throughout the district.

</div>

</section>
